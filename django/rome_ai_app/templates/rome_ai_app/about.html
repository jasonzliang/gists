About Rome AI Initiative

Connecting ancient wisdom with cutting-edge technology

Our Story

Founded in 2022, the Rome AI Initiative brings together researchers, engineers, and visionaries committed to advancing the field of artificial intelligence in the heart of the Eternal City.

Our unique approach combines the innovative spirit of ancient Roman engineering with modern computational techniques, creating AI solutions that respect human values while pushing the boundaries of what's possible.

Based in Rome's emerging technology district, our organization serves as a bridge between academia, industry, and government to foster collaboration and accelerate AI innovation throughout Italy and beyond.

Our Vision

A world where artificial intelligence empowers humanity, preserves cultural heritage, and solves complex global challenges through ethical, inclusive, and innovative approaches.

Our Mission

To advance the field of artificial intelligence through groundbreaking research, foster a vibrant AI ecosystem in Rome, and develop responsible AI solutions that benefit society while respecting human values.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our work and research

Innovation

We believe in pushing boundaries, challenging conventional wisdom, and exploring new frontiers in artificial intelligence.

Ethics

We are committed to developing AI systems that respect human autonomy, promote fairness, and prevent harm.

Collaboration

We foster partnerships across disciplines, sectors, and borders to accelerate innovation and solve complex problems.

Impact

We prioritize research and applications that address meaningful challenges and create positive societal impact.

Our Journey

Key milestones in the Rome AI Initiative story

January 2022

Foundation

Rome AI Initiative was established by a group of AI researchers from Rome's leading universities.

June 2022

First Research Lab

Opened our first research laboratory in Rome's technology district.

October 2022

Academic Partnerships

Established partnerships with Sapienza University, Roma Tre University, and Tor Vergata University.

March 2023

First AI Symposium

Hosted the first Rome AI Symposium, bringing together researchers from across Europe.

September 2023

Industry Partnerships

Launched collaborative research programs with leading technology companies.

January 2024

AI Education Initiative

Started our AI education program to train the next generation of AI researchers in Rome.
